Self Awareness

Self awareness can improve our judgement and help us identify opportunities for professional development and personal growth. For instance, where an extrovert see's a sales position as a great opportunity to exercise their 'extrovert-type behavior' and introvert would most likely run the other direction, away from such a position. Awareness of your personality helps find situations in which we will thrive. and avoid situations in which we will experience too much stress.

Personal Development

As you become more self aware, you begin to understand your personality traits, gain valuable insights into your strength, weaknesses, and preferences. This allows you to make better decisions, improve relationships and lead a more fulfilling life.

Relationship

Our personalities can have a significant impact on our social interactions and relationships with other. Understanding one's personality and that of others can facilitate empathy, communication, and the building of meaningful connection.

Career Development

Understanding your personality ype can help you determine what career you might enjoy and how well you might perform in certain job roles. Recognizing one's own strengths and weaknesses can lead to success in our career developement.
