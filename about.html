Purpose

The purpose of this website was to inform about the importance of personalities and how they impact many aspects of our lives. Neuroscience suggests that we are neurologically wired to connect with others. It is part of our nature to build relationships and making healthy connections impacts all dimensions of our well-being, including physical and mental health and our ability to flourish at work or every other facet of life.
